Using ArcPy.mapping to Export maps to PDF Map Books

Back

Export map document to PDF

>>> mxd = arcpy.mapping.MapDocument("CURRENT")
>>> arcpy.mapping.ExportToPDF(mxd,"C:/your_path/map1.pdf")

Zoom in to Denver downtown area. Export to another map.

>>> arcpy.mapping.ExportToPDF(mxd,"C:/your_path/map2.pdf")

Append your two maps to make a multiple page map book.

>>> PDFdoc = arcpy.mapping.PDFDocumentCreate(r"C:/your_path/Final.pdf")
>>> PDFdoc.appendPages("C:/your_path/map1.pdf")
>>> PDFdoc.appendPages("C:/your_path/map2.pdf")
>>> PDFdoc.saveAndClose()
